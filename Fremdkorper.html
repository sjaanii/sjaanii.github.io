Frëmdkörper

Fremdkörper is an interdisciplinary art project of Fontys school of Fine and Performing Arts. Fremdkörper is a collaboration between all first-year Artcode students, third/ fourth-year students of the Dance and Circus Academy, music producers/ DJ's from Rock Academy, artdirectors and choreographers. This club event consists short performances and club acts. This edition (2018) took place in Werkwarenhuis, Den Bosch and it was awesome! Later that year, a recap of the project was also shown in Supperclub, Amsterdam.

As an Artcode student I worked on visual content, styling and branding.
