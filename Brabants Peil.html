Brabants Peil

With this project I got the opportunity to make a design proposal for AA en Maas. The installation would indicate in real time what the average water levels are in four districts of Brabant. This data can be found at any time on their website. The water get pumped in and out reacting to the data. I made a design proposal for a spatial installation outside, visible from the highway, at the entrance hall and as piece of wall decor.
