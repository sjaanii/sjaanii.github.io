The Creation

In collaboration with my fellow students: Amber van der Kuij, Roos Stolk and Wout Peeters we were given the opportunity to collaborate with FHK AMPA and digital artist Ian Biscoe. The piece "Die Schöpfung" (a composition by Joseph Haydn) was played. This oratorio musically describes the biblical story of the Creation of Earth.
Location: The Cenakel Tilburg

Therefore we created visual content by using seven chapters:"the Seven Deadly Sins". As Artcoders we worked on content for each of our chosen chapters and AV build-up.

This page shows some of of the visual content I created for chapter "Lust".
