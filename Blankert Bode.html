Blankert Bode

Due to corona this year not a yearly family reunion, but a family newspaper.

We formed the editorial team together with my mom and auntie. The entire newspaper is made by and for the family. Beautiful interviews, articles, illustrations, archive pieces, puzzles and many photos were submitted. I collected all the content and put it togheter in this newspaper. Layout is inspired by NRC newspaper.

Second edition!

Because my grandfather turned 80 years old, a second edition was specially dedicated to him. We asked the family to submit input to map out his past 80 years.
