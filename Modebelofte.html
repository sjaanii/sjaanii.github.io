Modebelofte

In my first year at the Artcode Academy we've gotten the opportunity to shoot 360 video's for Modebelofte. Therefore me and my fellow student Laszlo Goudman got the chance to go to London for a few days to film three, freshly graduated, fashion designers. The video's give an insight in their workproces and daily lifes. Their graduatworks where exposed at Modebelofte, Dutch Design week 2017. Together with other fashion designers from all over the world.
The 360 video experience was shown with goggles as end-stop of the route. To play with interaction we experimented with duplications and voice-over in combination with locally recorded sound and their favorite music.
