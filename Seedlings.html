Seedlings

Seedlings is an explorational project about the intelligence and playfulness of plants. With this installation I tried to capture their seeking movements in a playful way. This work is an aesthetic representation of the explorational existence of a living being. The installation works interactive with light. This creates a connection between you and the plants. Just let them grow...

Stefano Mancuso.The roots of plant intelligence. 2010. Via Ted.
