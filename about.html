About

Hi there!

I always liked to make art, especially with computers. Throughout the years I became captivated by working with interactive technologies as an art medium. And now I mainly conduct research to the connections between us, nature and technology. I like to create playful and interactive work.

Background
First, I studied spatial and visual design in Utrecht, graduated in 2017. After that, I did my Bachelor of Design at the beautiful interdisciplinary Art's Academy in Tilburg. Graduated in 2022.

Since 2019 I work as all-round designer on various art projects.
With my studio I like to connect people. I organize art exhibitions, workshops for kids and other creative experiences.
