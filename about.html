About me

Hi there!

Throughout the years I became captivated by working with technologies as an art medium. Therefore I now mainly conduct research to the (dis)connections between people and technology. About it's history and it's future possibilities.

Interaction and experience design appeals to me. I like to create social and playful work. With a background in spatial and visual design I'm currently studying for my Bachelor of Design at the beautiful interdisciplinary Art's Academy in Tilburg. Surrounded by creativity I work on various projects that can offer playful explorations.

Since 2019 I started as a part-time freelancer by the name Visualicious. This name stands for delicious and playful creative output that aims to connect people.
